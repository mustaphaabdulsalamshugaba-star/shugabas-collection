Meet The CEO

Abdulsalam Shugaba Mustapha


Abdulsalam Shugaba Mustapha is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Shugaba's Collection. Born on the 1st of January 2006 in Magami, Gusau, Zamfara State, Nigeria, he is a young entrepreneur, business enthusiast and visionary leader. He is the son of Alhaji Mustapha Umar and Hajiya Khadijat Muhammad. Driven by a passion for fashion, entrepreneurship, innovation and excellence, he established Shugaba's Collection with the vision of building a globally recognized fashion brand. The company specializes in premium Jallabiya, Abaya, Caps, Atamfa, Shoes and Accessories, providing customers with quality products and excellent customer service. Beyond business, Abdulsalam is passionate about technology, cybersecurity, youth empowerment, community development and entrepreneurship. His long-term vision is to transform Shugaba's Collection into a leading international fashion and lifestyle brand.
